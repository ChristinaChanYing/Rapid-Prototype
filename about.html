About Us

The Montessori School, as a licensed private nursery by Ontario Ministry of Community and Social Services, provides a learning environment of Montessori method by recognizing the uniqueness of each child.

Our curriculums which follows The Association of Montessori International (AMI) help your children to grow up with independence, self-esteem, love of learning which your children will carry for a lifetime.

Optional extracurricular programs are also offered throughout the year.
