Programs

The Montessori School program is designed for children aged between 2.5 and 6 to explore a variety of human experience : language, mathematics, geography, science, music, art, history and culture.

All of our materials are carefully structured to approach a broad range of concepts from simple to the complex.

The teachers of Montessori School ae qualified and well-trained. They conduct each class with responsibilities on preparing and maintaining the daily lessons of curriculum for children.
