Education

Here at Montessori, “practical life” is essential to what we teach. It helps the child adapt and feel more comfortable to the outside world.

Children at Montessori School will develop a deep sense of:

  • self-development
  • self-esteem
  • independence
  • co-operation
  • good manners
  • mutual respect
  • responsibility
  • freedom
  • self-motivation
  • self-confidence
  • love of learning
